💳 Checkout — Payment Options

Secure payment, minimal data collection

All pages on this site use HTTPS with an SSL certificate issued by a trusted certificate authority. All payment information is transmitted over encrypted channels to prevent it from being intercepted or tampered with. Actual charges are processed by reputable payment providers such as PayPal, Stripe, Alipay / WeChat Pay / UnionPay. We only receive the payment result and do not store your card number, CVV, or other sensitive data.

💳 Pay with credit / debit card

For card payments we need your full shipping details here for delivery and verification. This is the most traditional and widely supported method.

Required fields in this tab Card · Full shipping info
  • Country / Region (for shipping availability & cost)
  • Recipient name & phone number
  • Full street address, city and postal / zip code

Why we ask for your phone number

For international shipments the carrier may need to contact you for customs clearance or final delivery. We only share this with the logistics providers handling your order.

When to choose card payments

Good if you prefer to pay directly with Visa / MasterCard and keep everything in one checkout flow without being redirected.

🅿️ Pay with PayPal (minimal typing)

For privacy and convenience, if you choose PayPal we rely on the shipping address already stored in your PayPal account whenever possible.

What we still need on this page (before redirecting to PayPal)

  • Country / Region — to show if we can ship to your location and estimate shipping fees.

What we do not ask you to repeat for PayPal

  • Full street address, city and postal code (we prefer to reuse your PayPal shipping address).
  • Card number, expiry date or CVV — these stay inside PayPal.

Why this can improve conversions

Users used to paying with PayPal often expect a quick flow. If we ask them to fill a full shipping form before redirecting, some may give up. Minimising typing here respects that habit.

💳 Pay with Stripe (minimal typing)

When you choose Stripe we process your card through Stripe's secure checkout. We aim to keep the form here minimal and let Stripe handle card details and extra verification.

What we still need on this page (before redirecting to Stripe)

  • Country / Region — to show if we can ship to your location and estimate shipping fees.

What we do not ask you to repeat for Stripe

  • Full card number, expiry date and CVV — these stay inside Stripe's checkout.

Why this can improve conversions

Many users are familiar with Stripe-powered card checkouts. Keeping most card details inside Stripe and reducing typing on our side can make the payment feel faster and more secure.

🌏 Pay with Alipay / WeChat Pay / UnionPay

For these local payment methods you will be redirected to the provider to authorise the payment. We usually still need your shipping details here to prepare the parcel.

Typically required in this tab

  • Country / Region + city and postal code
  • Recipient name and phone number (for local carriers).
  • Street address if not fully provided by the wallet.

Notes

In some regions these wallets can also return part of the shipping address. This demo keeps the explanation generic — we can refine it once the real API behaviour is confirmed.

🏦 Pay by bank transfer (on request)

How bank transfer works in this checkout

  • Include your order number as the transfer reference so we can match your payment quickly.

🌍 Pay with WorldFirst

For selected international or business orders you can pay via WorldFirst. You pay to a local account in your currency, and WorldFirst settles the funds to us with an additional service fee.

Typical WorldFirst flow

  • We show you a WorldFirst account in your region and currency after you choose this option.
  • You initiate the transfer from your bank to WorldFirst, using the order reference we provide.
