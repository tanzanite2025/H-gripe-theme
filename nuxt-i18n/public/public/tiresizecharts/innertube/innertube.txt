🔧 Pump Compatibility
- Standard pumps: Suitable for ≤45mm rims, 40–60mm valves work fine.
- Locking-head pumps: Better for mid/high rims, securely grip shorter valves.
- Valve extenders: Essential for ≥70mm rims, especially inner-type extenders for airtightness.

⚠️ Notes
- Rule of thumb: Valve length should exceed rim height by at least 15mm to ensure secure pump connection.
- Valve extenders:
- External type: Easy to install, but less airtight.
- Internal type: Requires removing valve core, better airtightness, recommended for tubeless setups.
- Brand differences: Continental and Schwalbe support internal extenders; cheaper inner tubes often do not.
