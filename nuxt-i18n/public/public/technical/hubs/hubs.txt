J-bend (Elbowed Spokes)
-The spoke head is bent to fit into the hub flange holes.
-Advantages: Highly compatible, easy to service.
-Disadvantages: Slightly lower spoke tension, more traditional appearance.
Straight-pull
-The spokes are inserted directly into the hub without bending.
-Common hole count: 24H (arranged in 1:1 or 2:1 patterns).
Advantages: Higher spoke tension, cleaner aesthetics.
Disadvantages: Limited compatibility when replacing spokes, less convenient for long-distance maintenance.
